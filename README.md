Name: Alchemy World

game concept: explore, craft, and survive the until time runs out.

controls:
- wasd to move
- space to jump
- E to pick up items
- F to craft
- Q to eat apples

gameplay systems:
- crafting system
- inventory
- hunger bar
- survival timer

Known issues:
- crafting menu does not show up when player presses F near cauldron
- OnEat function in HungerScripts doesn’t work
- Audio does not work for music or sound effects
